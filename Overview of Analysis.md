\# Power BI Sales Insight Project Explanation



\## 1. Problem Statement



The company was facing difficulty in **understanding its sales performance** due to large volumes of raw transactional data stored across different sources.

Key challenges included:



\* No clear view of **overall revenue and profit**

\* Difficulty identifying **top-performing products, customers, and regions**

\* Lack of insight into **sales trends over time**

\* Inability to detect **loss-making markets and products**

\* Manual reporting that was **time-consuming and error-prone**



* **Objective:**

To build an **interactive Power BI dashboard** that converts raw sales data into **actionable business insights** to support management decision-making.



---



\## 2. Data Overview



The dataset contains sales transaction records with the following key attributes:



\* Order Date

\* Product Category \& Product Name

\* Customer Name

\* Market / Region

\* Sales Quantity

\* Revenue

\* Cost \& Profit



The data was cleaned and transformed using **Power Query**, including:



\* Removing duplicates

\* Handling missing values

\* Creating calculated columns (Profit, Profit Margin)



---



\## 3. Dashboard Design \& Visualizations



The dashboard is designed in a **clean, business-friendly layout** and includes:



\###  KPI Cards



\* **Total Revenue**

\* **Total Sales Quantity**

\* **Total Profit**

\* **Profit Margin**



* These provide an **instant snapshot** of overall performance.



---



\### Sales Trend Analysis (Line Chart)



\* Revenue trend over **months and years**



-> Helps identify:



\* Growth patterns

\* Seasonal demand

\* Peak and low-performing periods



---



\###  Product-wise Analysis (Bar / Column Chart)



\* Revenue and profit by **product category**

\* Comparison of **sales quantity vs profit**



-> Helps identify:



\* Best-selling products

\* Products with **high sales but low profit**



---



\### Customer-wise Analysis (Table / Bar Chart)



\* Top customers by revenue and profit



-> Helps:



\* Identify **high-value customers**

\* Detect customers with **low profit margins**



---



\###  Region / Market-wise Analysis (Map / Bar Chart)



\* Sales and profit by **region**



-> Helps:



\* Identify strong markets

\* Detect regions with **high revenue but losses**



---



\###  Filters \& Slicers



\* Year

\* Region

\* Product Category



* Enables **dynamic analysis** and drill-down insights.
