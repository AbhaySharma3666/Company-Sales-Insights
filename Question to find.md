Below are \*\*analysis questions\*\* generated for a \*\*Power BI Sales Insights project\*\*, framed in a manner suitable for \*\*semester exams, project reviews, viva voce, and interviews\*\*.  

These questions are based on \*\*typical Power BI visualizations\*\* such as KPI cards, bar charts, line charts, maps, tables, and slicers.



---



\## 1. Overall Sales Performance (KPI Cards)



\*\*Q1.\*\* What is the \*\*total revenue\*\*, \*\*total sales quantity\*\*, and \*\*total profit\*\*, and how do they compare with the previous period?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Helps evaluate overall business performance.



\*\*Q2.\*\* Is the business achieving its \*\*sales targets\*\*, or is there a shortfall or excess?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Measures success against organizational goals.



---



\## 2. Sales Trend Analysis (Line / Area Chart)



\*\*Q3.\*\* How has \*\*sales revenue changed over time\*\* (monthly or yearly)?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Identifies growth patterns, decline, or seasonality.



\*\*Q4.\*\* Which months show \*\*peak sales\*\* and which show the \*\*lowest sales\*\*?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Useful for demand forecasting and inventory planning.



---



\## 3. Product-wise Analysis (Bar / Column Chart)



\*\*Q5.\*\* Which \*\*product categories\*\* contribute the \*\*highest revenue\*\*?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Identifies top-performing products.



\*\*Q6.\*\* Are there products with \*\*high sales quantity but low profit\*\*?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Indicates pricing, discounting, or cost-related issues.



---



\## 4. Customer-wise Analysis (Table / Bar Chart)



\*\*Q7.\*\* Who are the \*\*top 5 customers\*\* by revenue?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Helps focus on high-value customers.



\*\*Q8.\*\* Are there customers generating \*\*high revenue but low profit margins\*\*?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Indicates the need for renegotiation or cost optimization.



---



\## 5. Region / Market-wise Analysis (Map / Bar Chart)



\*\*Q9.\*\* Which \*\*region or market\*\* generates the \*\*maximum sales and profit\*\*?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Identifies strong geographical markets.



\*\*Q10.\*\* Are there regions with \*\*high sales but negative or low profit\*\*?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Highlights operational or logistical inefficiencies.



---



\## 6. Profitability Analysis (Profit Margin Visuals)



\*\*Q11.\*\* What is the \*\*overall profit margin\*\*, and how does it vary across products or regions?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Shows long-term business sustainability.



\*\*Q12.\*\* Which products or markets are \*\*loss-making\*\*?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Helps decide product discontinuation or strategy improvement.



---



\## 7. Time-based Comparison (Year / Quarter Slicer)



\*\*Q13.\*\* How does \*\*current year sales\*\* compare with the \*\*previous year\*\*?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Measures year-on-year growth.



\*\*Q14.\*\* Is profit growing at the \*\*same rate as sales\*\*?  

\*\*Insight:\*\* Detects increasing costs or declining efficiency.



---



\## 8. Decision-Oriented Business Questions



\*\*Q15.\*\* Which products and regions should the company \*\*focus on to maximize profit\*\*?



\*\*Q16.\*\* Where should the company \*\*reduce costs or revise pricing strategies\*\*?



\*\*Q17.\*\* What insights can help management \*\*increase revenue in low-performing markets\*\*?
